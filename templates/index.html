What is Clinical Trial Trend Detector?

Our tool helps researchers, healthcare professionals, and analysts understand the relationship between published medical research and ongoing clinical trials. By analyzing both scientific papers and clinical trials, we identify trends, gaps, and opportunities in medical research.
